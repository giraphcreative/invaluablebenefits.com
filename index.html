A home equity loan is a product that allows you to use the equity of your home as collateral. The loan can be closed-end with a fixed interest rate and set end date where you'll stop making payments, or a revolving line of credit that operates much like a credit card. Because the rate is significantly lower than what you'll find on even the most competitive credit cards, home equity products are great for:

  • Affordably making home improvements
  • Consolidating debt
  • Making a major purchase like college tuition or vacation
